If you want to, you can enter your daily weight and track it.

You can enter your weight in either pounds (lbs) or kilograms (kg). The app will convert from one unit to the other.

Also, you can enter your weight in DietWZ and have it show in your iPhone Health app. Or, you can set DietWZ to read your weight from the entries you make in your Health app. To set these preferences, go to your phone's Privacy settings, select the Health app, then select DietWZ and chose the option that best suits your needs.

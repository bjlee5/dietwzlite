You can collaborate or compete with friends and acquaintances using DietWZ. Tap the "Friends" icon, then the "Add" button on the Groups screen. Enter a group name to get started. Tap "Save" to create the group.

Next, use the buttons to select what information you want to share.

To add friends to your group, tap the "Invite" button and enter an email address. When you tap "Invite", your email app will come up and you can edit the text of the message. (And, if you have more than one email account on your mobile device, you can select which account the invitation will be sent from.)

Have fun working with or competing with your friends!

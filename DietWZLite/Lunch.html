Normal: 1 Point
Light: 2 Points
Excessive: 0 Points
Skip: NEGATIVE 1 Point (Don’t skip meals – it’s not healthy.)

What’s normal? A meal with about 1/3 of your daily requirement for calories and daily recommended nutrients.

Light: About 1/4 of your daily calories requirement, but still with some of your daily recommended nutrients. Low-fat and low-calorie choices are usually part of a light meal.

Excessive: More than 1/3 of your daily calories requirement.

Think about your overall diet at mid-day and make choices appropriately. If your day looks like it will be low physical activity OR if you had an excessive breakfast – have a light lunch!

Add some fruits or veggies to your lunch. Each serving is one point!

Don't have an idea of your daily calories objective? The BEST source for nutrition information is a registered dietitian or your physician. If you can’t do that, check out the DietWZ website.

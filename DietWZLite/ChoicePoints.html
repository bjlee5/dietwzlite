Make healthy food and beverage choices and get some exercise over each 2-day cycle to score points!

There's no hard rule about how many points to score -- YOU decide what your points goal should be, based on your objectives.

For example, a healthy eating and exercise score over a 2-day cycle might be in the range of 16 points or more -- based on normal meals, daily exercise, and 4 to 5 servings of fruits and vegetables each day.

Details of the points associated with your choices are shown for each day in a 2-day cycle.

The past 15 2-day cycles are also shown.

The More History selection provides additional graphs showing how you've been doing with your choices in each category for the past 30 days. AND, you can see the history of your custom categories.

You can use the graphs to see trends in your eating habits and spot areas where you might be able to improve!

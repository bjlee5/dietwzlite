You can create up to 10 (ten) custom categories to track - anything you want! For each category, you can select one of three types:

  • Yes/No question. (Example: Took Vitamin D today?)
  • Counter. (Example: Cups of coffee:)
  • Numeric entry. (Example: Added sugar for the day:)

To set up something to track, you need to enter the text for that item.

For a counter, you can select a maximum value (or just leave the default value, 99).

For a numeric entry, you can select how many decimal places you want to the right of the decimal point.

To delete a category, left swipe it in the Categories list screen, then tap the Delete button, OR tap that item in the Categories list screen, and the Edit Categories screen will display. There is a Delete button on that screen.

To edit a category, tap that item in the Categories list screen, and the Edit Categories screen will display.

DietWZ will track your custom category information to show you the history of the last 30 days. Tap on the 2-Day Cycle Choice Points display on the Home screen, tap More History at the bottom of the Choice Points screen, and scroll down to see your categories.

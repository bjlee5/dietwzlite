For men, not more than 2 servings per day.

For women, not more than 1 serving per day.

Anything over these amounts = NEGATIVE 1 point PER SERVING (to a maximum of -3)

Moderation is the only reasonable option IF you use alcohol. And, if you don’t use alcohol, don’t start using it for any of the possible health benefits unless advised to do so by your doctor.

Examples of single servings of alcohol:

  • Beer: 12 fluid ounces (oz) (355 milliliters, ml)
  • Wine: 5 oz (148 ml)
  • Distilled spirits (80 proof): 1.5 oz (44 ml)
